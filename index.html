Bio

Xiaozhou Zhang (张小舟), born in Hefei, China, is an autonomous vehicle software engineer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. He received his master's degree in Robotics and Computer & Information Science from the University of Pennsylvania in 2021. Before that, he finished his undergrad in Mechanical Engineering at Southwest Jiaotong University in 2018.

Journey

2019-2021

Xiaozhou was a developer at F1TENTH, an international autonomous racing car community. He also served as the planning algorithm leader of a UPenn's crew in the 5th F1TENTH Autonomous Racing Competition.

2018-2019

Xiaozhou was a developer from project Quori, a low-cost socially interactive robot platform hosted by ModLab, which was presented at Designs for Different Futures, an exhibit at the Philadelphia Museum of Art in 2019.

2011-2014

Xiaozhou was the head leader of the VEX Robotics team in Hefei No.8 Senior High School and served as both a programmer and a constructor. He and his team won the first prize in the 12th China Adolescent Robotics Competition and the gold award in Asia Pacific VEX Robotics Championship (New Zealand).
